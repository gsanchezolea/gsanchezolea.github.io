About Me

The beginning...

Since I can remember I loved technology, so much that my parents got me a Super Nintendo and Super Mario Bros. was my first game. After that, I got myself and old Tandy computer from Radioshack in which I would spend hours digging through it. As I got older I would be the guy to have all the newest and sometimes weird gadgets, and that is still who I am today.
